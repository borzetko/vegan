Substitutions


Being vegan bring oppoturtinies to try something new. You don´t have to be vegan to try soy milk though. But if you are new in plant based diet, it can be little confusing.

Milk

There is soy, oat,coconut,cashew, almond milk and even more. What is better – you can prepare them easily at home. It´s way more cheaper,it´s takes 5 minutes and they are really delicious.Cherry on top: You are also producing less waste.But you can easily get plant milk in any shop, with different prices and flavours.







Cheese

You can also get all kinds of cheeses in a shop.You can go with slices, blocks, shreds etc..They are based on soy milk and coconut milk.You can barely feel difference between vegan cheese and dairy cheese...

Butter

I found this butter with coconut shea in a shop near normal butters . It taste exactly like a normal butter and it can be even cheaper alternative .You can add this for anything ( baking , on the toast..)

Eggs

It´s really easy to replace eggs while preparing cake or while cooking.Somethimes it can be tricky to get that right combination of texture and volume while something that will also behave in a similar .You can use for example chia seeds, applesauce, banans, purees,silken tofu. If you like scrambled eggs- mash one package of tofu add black salt (this is the most important part)and other spices by your choice – it´s really great and you can not even tell the difference.?

Meat

You can substitute meat in every possible way . There is tofu, tempeh, seitan,soy,vegetable protein, vegan meat (i am not kidding), jackfruit. You can buy hams, sausages, or even vegan bacon.And egain, you can barely feel diference.
