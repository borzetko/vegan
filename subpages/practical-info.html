What is veganism ?

It means avoiding (you can look but shouldn´t eat them) all animal products- meat, eggs ,dairy products and also honey. When I tell somebody about veganism, most of the time the response is the same: I love cheese/meat/eggs so much, I can´t go fully vegan even though I want to.
